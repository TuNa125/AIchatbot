How to Protect Yourself

Prevention is always better than cure, so here are some ways to help prevent getting the top diseases in Malaysia.

Wear Mask

COVID-19 is commonly spread is via droplets spread through the air from person to person. By wearing a mask, it decreases the ability for droplets to get from person to person.

Wash Your Hands

Washing your hands frequently such as washing your hands every single time before and after eating ensures that there is lesser chance of diseases getting into your body

Stay at Home

Staying at home means crowded areas are avoided and it greatly reduce the chances of getting infected by diseases that spread through the air.
